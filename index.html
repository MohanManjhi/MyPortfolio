The Story Behind the Code

From spotting real-world problems to shipping production-grade products

I build products people actually use ✨

I'm Mohan Manjhi, a Full Stack Engineer and Founder & CEO of Vynorix Technologies. My journey started when I noticed patients waiting hours in crowded clinics with no idea when their turn would come. That frustration sparked Token Care — a smart OPD queue management system now bringing order to chaotic waiting rooms across India.

Beyond Token Care, I've built 200+ free online tools on Ira Magic Tools, a multilingual AI voice assistant for government schemes, and an AI-powered SQL chatbot. I also won the Microsoft BhashaBandhu Regional Hackathon and have solved 400+ DSA problems across platforms.

I thrive on the 0-to-1 journey — from architecting multi-tenant SaaS platforms to deploying production systems on AWS. I don't just write code; I ship products that serve real users.
